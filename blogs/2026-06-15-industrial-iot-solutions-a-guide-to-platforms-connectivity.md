Getting an industrial IoT solution right depends on decisions made at three distinct layers: connectivity, data architecture, and platform. The guide argues MQTT has become the standard IIoT messaging protocol for constrained networks, and that pairing a Unified Namespace with Event-Driven Architecture turns raw operational data into trustworthy, AI-ready enterprise intelligence by owning the data layer rather than relying on platforms layered atop existing infrastructure.
